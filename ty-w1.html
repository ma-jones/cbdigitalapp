Timber yard London W1

Followings on from Timber Yard's first installation (Old Street) they had a new venture, Timber Yard (Convent Garden). This included a cinema projection to their meeting-room that also included a media-room for meetings. The kit included a Yamaha receiver, Yamaha Blu-Ray DVD, Polk 5.1 speaker system. The equipment also included a Optoma projector and acoustically transparent screen that let the sound from the speakers perform properly.
