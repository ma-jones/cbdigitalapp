Luxury Apartment - Victoria

CB Digital completely reconfigured and interior designed this dated three bedroom apartment in Victoria into a four bedroom luxury family home. Via structural reconfiguration, an overhaul of the existing materials and installation of beautiful bespoke furniture, high-end finishes and state of the art audio-visual systems throughout, the apartment was transformed into a light, bright and usable family space.
