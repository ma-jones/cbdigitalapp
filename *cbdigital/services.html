Home Cinema

Just imagine your own dedicated Home cinema providing you with a realistic theatre going experience in the comfort of your own home. With a front or rear projection a very large screen size can be achieved. As the picture is only part of the overall effect, the correct speaker system, DVD player, Amps and lighting, also play a huge part in creating the right results.

We can help with the design and planning to create your own Cinema at home. Basic packages include stereo systems going through to high end state of the art technology complex 5.1 or 7.1 surround systems, we will ensure you maximise your budget and get the best out of the equipment. Our installation service includes full and comprehensive demonstration of the equipment giving you the confidence to use it once we have left.

Using state of the art technology here at CB Digital Ltd Home Installations we can help you create a home cinema for your home. Home cinema is a set of AV equipment connected and calibrated together to give you movie theatre quality sound in your home. Our experts can advise on the best system for your requirements and room size.
